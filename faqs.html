Frequently Asked Questions
  • How do I connect my digital wallet?

    1. Making sure you have a digital wallet first, if don't, please register one of the followings:

    Metamask WalletConnect

    After you successfully registered your own digital wallet, please remember to install the browser plug-in provided by the wallet or you will not be able to connect your wallet.

    2. Login your account, click your avatar, click "connect" under "my wallet", follow the connecting instruction, then you are good to go.
